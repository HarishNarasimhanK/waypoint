# Waypoint

**Every goal needs a waypoint.**

A waypoint is a significant point on a journey that helps people navigate toward their goals. Waypoint helps users discover, evaluate, and reach their next opportunity—whatever that may be.

---

## What is Waypoint?

Waypoint is a web application that solves a painful problem for off-campus job seekers: **finding fresh job postings early, evaluating them quickly, and managing applications without losing track.**

### Current Scope (v0.1 — Job Radar + Resume Optimizer)

**Job Radar**
- Aggregates job listings from multiple platforms into a single, clean feed
- Displays jobs with company, role, freshness, and relevance score
- Click into any job for the full description, qualifications, and details — nothing missed
- Direct link to apply on the original job portal
- Filter by role, company, location, and freshness

**Resume Optimizer**
- Per-job resume analysis — upload your resume and compare it against a specific job description
- Get a match score and actionable suggestions (keywords to add, bullets to reword)
- Subtle, optimized tweaks — not a full rewrite
- Pluggable AI backend: free (open-source models) or premium (paid APIs)

**Application Tracking**
- Simple status management: New → Viewed → Applied → Interviewing
- Auto-marks as "Applied" when you click the apply link
- Never lose track of where you stand

---

## Project Philosophy

- **Incremental delivery** — ship working features, then extend
- **Extensible architecture** — pluggable AI providers, job board adapters, notification channels
- **Free-first** — core functionality works with fully open-source tools; premium features use paid APIs
- **Simple UX** — a fresher should get value within 30 seconds of opening the app
